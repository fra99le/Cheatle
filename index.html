Instructions:

  1. Enter the current day identifier (easily be obtained from one of your friend's scores).
  2. Enter the length of the word for the day.
  3. Enter the maxium number of tries for the day.
  4. Check the hard mode box, for an extra bit of flair (optional).
  5. Copy and paste the resulting score string into a social media post.

Alternatively, you can just copy and paste someone else's score, changing the number before the / to a 1 and removing all of the rows of boxes except the bottom all green one.

For more information, see the project at https://github.com/fra99le/Cheatle.
